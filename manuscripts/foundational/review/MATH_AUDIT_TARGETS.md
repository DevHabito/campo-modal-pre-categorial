# Mathematical Audit Targets

## Target 1 — Proposition 2.2

Check that continuous strictly increasing marginals and the probability
integral transform justify the statement that the finite coordinatewise-order
law depends at most on the copula. Confirm that the text does not assert
injectivity in the copula.

## Target 2 — Proposition 2.3

Check the fiberwise identifiability criterion and its extension from one
finite observation to repeated independent order observations.

## Target 3 — Proposition 3.1

Assess whether the information criterion for an added mark is correctly
phrased as necessary, not sufficient for empirical identification.

## Target 4 — Proposition 4.2

Check that the standardized family

\[
K_f(i,j)
=
\frac{A_{ij}f(z_{ij})}{\sum_kA_{ik}f(z_{ik})}
\]

really provides infinitely many distinct local, relabeling-equivariant,
positive-affine-invariant kernels.

## Target 5 — Proposition 4.3

Check domain and regularity assumptions in the step from
\(f(x)/f(y)=g(x-y)\) to the positive continuous exponential solutions.

## Target 6 — Lemma 4.4

Check that extension stability, positive-affine numerical invariance,
continuity, and \(D(x,x)=0\) force the trivial score difference. Identify any
missing antisymmetry or domain assumption.

## Target 7 — Section 5.4

Check that the finitely branching path-space construction and cylinder
premeasure satisfy the hypotheses of the cited extension theorem.

## Target 8 — Proposition 5.2

Check exact clone-refinement invariance, including the dependence of
\(T_\nu\) only on the marked measure and marked point.

## Target 9 — Propositions 6.1 and 7.1

Check mass normalization, affine covariance, and the transport identity

\[
Q_\lambda'
=
(1-a)\bar q_\mu+aQ_{a\lambda}.
\]

## Target 10 — Proposition 7.2

Independently verify the rational four-point witness and confirm that it proves
failure of closure only for the declared macrostate on the unrestricted finite
class.

## Target 11 — Section 8.4 and Proposition 8.1

Check the distinction between occupancy-specific aggregation and strong
lumpability, and verify the exact flow formula.

## Target 12 — Section 9

Check whether the phrase “relative minimality” is justified by the documented
obstructions and sufficiently restricted to the operations and
representations studied.
