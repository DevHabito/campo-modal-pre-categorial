**Informational Requirements for Pre-Categorical Relational Models:
Identifiability, Measure, Dynamic Closure, and Coarse-Graining**

## Review requested

Please evaluate the manuscript as a formal mathematical-foundations article,
not as a completed physical theory. The central questions are:

1. Are every proposition's assumptions sufficient and stated?
2. Are classical results attributed at the correct strength?
3. Is the four-point nonclosure witness exact and logically sufficient?
4. Does the distinction between static sufficiency and dynamic closure hold?
5. Is the occupancy/lumpability argument stated precisely?
6. Is the relative-minimality claim appropriately conditional?
7. Are any conclusions stronger than their proofs?
