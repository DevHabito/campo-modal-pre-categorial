# Manuscript C — Reference Plan

## Mandatory source families

- classical enumeration of finite topologies/preorders;
- finite posets and labeled-poset counts;
- SCC condensation and transitive closure;
- restricted growth functions or set-partition encodings;
- dynamic SCC and reachability sensitivity;
- strong bridges/dominators where relevant;
- average sensitivity of graph algorithms.

## Citation rule

The manuscript must distinguish:

1. a primary source for a theorem;
2. a modern accessible source used to verify the theorem;
3. the project's independent implementation.

The repository's C1 audit is evidence of the search and comparison, not a
substitute for citing the original mathematical literature.
