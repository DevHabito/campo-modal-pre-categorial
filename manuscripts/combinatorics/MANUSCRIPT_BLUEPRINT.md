# Manuscript C — Combinatorics Blueprint

## Working title

**Condensation Preorders of Labeled Digraphs: Complete and Representative
Encodings, Exact Enumeration, and Edge-Toggle Sensitivity**

## One-sentence thesis

Complete labeled reachability preorders and minimum-representative
quotient-poset codes are distinct combinatorial objects; their exact
enumerations, encoding fibers, and response to a single edge toggle can be
studied without any physical interpretation.

## Contribution hierarchy

### Attributed background

- SCC condensation produces a quotient partial order.
- The number of labeled preorders is
  \[
  \sum_k S(n,k)p(k).
  \]
- The value \(6942\) at \(n=5\) is classical.

### Project-specific exact results

- formal definition of the minimum-representative code;
- exact count
  \[
  N_{\mathrm{rep}}(n)=
  \sum_k\binom{n-1}{k-1}p(k);
  \]
- proof and explicit mechanism of non-injectivity;
- complete n=5 fiber accounting;
- exact definition and values of edge-toggle sensitivity
  \[
  P_2=1,\quad P_3=\frac34,\quad P_4=\frac12,\quad
  P_5=\frac{75}{256}.
  \]

### Blocking missing result

At least one structural theorem about \(P_n\), such as:

- a pivotal-toggle decomposition;
- an exact recurrence;
- a nontrivial upper or lower bound;
- a formula conditional on SCC type or edge density;
- an asymptotic result.

## Intended audience

Enumerative combinatorics, graph theory, finite orders, dynamic reachability,
and graph sensitivity.

## Forbidden framing

- emergent spacetime;
- physical time or distance;
- RZS phenomenology;
- \(6942\) as a new enumeration;
- \(75/256\) as a universal constant;
- categorical priority claims unsupported by the C1 search.
