## Excluded claims and reasons

MF-R012–MF-R015 form a possible future finite-poset/ordinal-observable paper.
They do not strengthen the encoding-and-sensitivity narrative and would make
the present manuscript look like an audit anthology.

### Physical language

The combinatorial ensemble is uniform over loopless labeled digraphs. No
argument currently connects that ensemble to nature. Therefore the paper must
not describe \(P_n\) as physical robustness, causal stability, or emergent
spacetime sensitivity.
